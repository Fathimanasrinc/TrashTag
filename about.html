Haritha Karma Sena

Empowering Communities for a Greener Tomorrow

Who We Are

Haritha Karma Sena is a team of trained waste management workers under the Local Self-Government Department (LSGD) of Kerala. We work towards efficient waste collection, recycling, and environmental conservation.

Our Mission

  • Ensure effective waste collection from homes and businesses.
  • Promote recycling and sustainable waste management.
  • Educate communities on eco-friendly waste disposal practices.
  • Support green initiatives for a cleaner environment.

What We Do

Waste Collection

We collect segregated waste from households and commercial spaces for proper disposal.

Recycling & Management

We promote recycling and composting to minimize landfill waste.

Community Awareness

We conduct programs to educate people on responsible waste management.
